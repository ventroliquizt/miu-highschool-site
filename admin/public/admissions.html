Admissions Process

Our straightforward 5-step process makes joining MIS simple and transparent

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with student and family information

  • Online application form
  • Student information
  • Family background
  • Academic history
2

Provide Documents

Upload required documents including academic records and recommendations

  • Previous school records
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport copies
  • Recommendation letters
3

Student Assessment

Participate in age-appropriate assessments and family interview

  • Academic assessment
  • Family interview
  • Student observation
  • English proficiency test
4

Receive Decision

Get admission decision within 10 business days after assessment

  • 10-day processing
  • Official letter
  • Welcome package
  • Next steps guide
5

Complete Enrollment

Finalize enrollment with payment and complete orientation process

  • Tuition payment
  • Medical forms
  • Orientation session
  • Uniform ordering
