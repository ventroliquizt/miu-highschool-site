Admissions Process Online Admissions Tuition & Fees

1

Inquiry & Information Gathering

Start by learning about our school programs, curriculum, and community. We encourage prospective families to explore our website and attend our information sessions.

  • Attend an Open House or Information Session
  • Review our academic programs and curriculum
  • Contact our admissions office for any questions
  • Schedule a campus tour (optional)
2

Application Submission

Submit the complete application package including all required documents. We accept applications year-round, but early application is recommended.

  • Complete the online application form
  • Submit student's academic records (last 2 years)
  • Provide birth certificate or passport copy
  • Submit 2 recent passport-sized photos
  • Include teacher recommendations (if applicable)
3

Assessment & Interview

Students are invited for an assessment and interview to ensure appropriate placement and to understand their educational needs.

  • Academic assessment (varies by grade level)
  • Student interview with academic staff
  • Parent interview with admissions team
  • English language proficiency test (if applicable)
4

Admission Decision & Enrollment

Receive admission decision and complete the enrollment process to secure your place at Mongolian International School.

  • Admission decision within 2 weeks
  • Submit enrollment agreement and fees
  • Complete health and medical forms
  • Attend new student orientation

Online Admissions

Streamlined digital application process for your convenience

Apply Online

Our online admissions portal makes it easy to apply from anywhere in the world. The secure platform allows you to track your application status and submit documents electronically.

The application process typically takes 20-30 minutes to complete. You can save your progress and return to complete it later.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of student's birth certificate or passport
  • Academic transcripts from previous school (last 2 years)
  • Health and immunization records
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of parent/guardian ID or passport
  • Teacher recommendations (for grades 6-12)
  • Any standardized test scores (if available)

Apply

Start your online application today. Our admissions team is available to assist you throughout the process.
