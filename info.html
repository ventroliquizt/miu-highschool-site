Common Questions

What is the application deadline for the upcoming academic year?

Applications are accepted year-round, but we recommend applying by April 30th for the following academic year starting in August. Early applications receive priority consideration for admissions and scholarship opportunities.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include:
1. Completed application form
2. Previous two years of academic transcripts
3. Birth certificate or passport copy
4. Health and immunization records
5. Two passport-sized photographs
6. Teacher recommendations (for grades 6-12)
7. Any standardized test scores (if available)

Is there an entrance exam for admission?

Yes, students in grades 3-12 are required to take an entrance assessment that includes English language proficiency and mathematics. For younger students (grades 1-2), a developmental assessment is conducted through observation and interactive activities.

What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow an international curriculum that combines the best practices of American and British educational systems, while also incorporating Mongolian language and culture studies. Our high school program offers Advanced Placement (AP) courses and prepares students for the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme.

What languages are taught at the school?

English is the primary language of instruction. We also offer comprehensive Mongolian language and literature classes. Additional language options include Mandarin Chinese, Russian, and Korean from middle school onwards. Spanish and French are available as electives in high school.

Do you offer Advanced Placement (AP) courses?

Yes, we offer 12 Advanced Placement courses including AP Calculus, AP Biology, AP Chemistry, AP Physics, AP World History, AP English Literature, and AP Computer Science. Our AP pass rate consistently exceeds the global average, with 85% of our students scoring 3 or higher.

What payment options are available for tuition?

We offer several payment options:
1. Full payment by August 1st (2% discount)
2. Two-installment plan (August and January)
3. Ten-month payment plan (August through May)
4. Credit card payments with additional processing fee
Payments can be made via bank transfer, cash, or credit card.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes, we offer sibling discounts:
• 10% discount for the second child
• 15% discount for the third child
• 20% discount for the fourth and subsequent children
Discounts apply to tuition only, not to other fees.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding academic achievement, as well as need-based financial aid. Scholarship applications must be submitted by March 31st for the following academic year. Awards range from 25% to full tuition coverage based on demonstrated need and merit.

How does the school communicate with parents?

We use multiple communication channels:
• Parent portal for grades, attendance, and announcements
• Weekly email newsletters
• Regular parent-teacher conferences
• School mobile app for updates and notifications
• Emergency alerts via SMS
• Social media updates on Facebook and Instagram
