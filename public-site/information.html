Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is the application deadline?

Applications are accepted year-round, but we recommend applying by April 30th for the following academic year starting in August.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include:

  • Completed application form
  • Previous academic transcripts (2 years)
  • Birth certificate or passport copy
  • Parent/guardian identification
  • Teacher recommendations
Is there an entrance exam?

Yes, students in grades 3-12 take an entrance assessment including:

  • English language proficiency test
  • Mathematics assessment
  • Reading comprehension (for grades 6-12)
  • Writing sample (for grades 9-12)
What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow an international curriculum combining:

  • American Common Core Standards for English and Math
  • British curriculum for Sciences
  • Advanced Placement (AP) courses for high school
  • Mongolian language and cultural studies
  • English language programs
Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer several scholarship opportunities:

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: For top-performing students
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: For families with financial constraints
  • Sports Scholarship: For exceptional athletes

Applications must be submitted by March 31st each year.
